## Tips for a better experience

- Use the **Theme** toolbar control to preview light and dark modes instantly.
- Switch preview **Backgrounds** to test components on canvas, surface, or brand-heavy backdrops.
- Explore the **Docs** tab on each story for usage notes, props tables, and accessibility considerations.
- Keep examples token-driven—avoid hard-coding colours or spacing in your own stories.
